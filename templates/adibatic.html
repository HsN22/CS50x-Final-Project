Calculate for an adibatic system, the final enthalpy, given pressure in bar and final temperature in celcius

For an Adibatic system, the Entropy is constant throught the process. This means it will be the same at the starting pressure and the final temperature, if you are given them.

Steps:

1. Calculate the Dryness Fraction, \( x_{1} \), at \( h_1 \)

2. Since process is Adibatic, \( x_{1} \) is the same as \( x_{2} \)

3. Desired property, i.e Enthalpy, \( h \), can be calculated

Specific Entropy: \[ s_1 = s_f + x_1 s_{fg} \]
\[ x_1 = x_2 \] Specific Enthalpy: \[ h_2 = h_f + x_2 s_{fg} \]
