This website can be used to perform calculations to find various thermodynamic properties.

Calculations on the Saturated tables that can be performed are, finding Saturated Pressure, Specific Volume of Vapor and a constant Entropy problem.

Calculations that can be performed on the Superheated and Supercritical tables are linearly interpolating Temperature, Specific Volume, Internal Energy, Specific Enthalpy and Specific Entropy, as not all values of a property for a given Temperature can be found manually by looking at the tables itself.

Thermodynamic properties come from two sources: NIST website for saturated steam properties and Roger & Mayhew tables for Superheated and Supercritical data. Saturated pressure and temperature are from NIST due to ease of digital access, while R&M would be ideal, as both sources are nearly identical for saturated data.
